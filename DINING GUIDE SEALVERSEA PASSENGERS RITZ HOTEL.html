The Ritz-Carlton sits at the heart of El Golf, Santiago's most prestigious neighbourhood — with Isidora Goyenechea, one of the city's finest dining streets, right on the hotel's doorstep. Walkable options span casual Italian to refined French bistro and award-winning Nikkei; a short taxi ride opens up the MUT market complex and the broader Vitacura dining corridor. No advance reservations required for most venues, though we always recommend calling ahead for dinner at upscale restaurants. Restaurants marked 🇨🇱 offer an authentic taste of Chilean cuisine.

Zone One
Isidora Goyenechea — Barrio El Golf
📍 Isidora Goyenechea, Las Condes 🚶 Walkable · 3–8 min on foot from The Ritz-Carlton

Known locally as the "milla gastronómica," Isidora Goyenechea runs parallel to the hotel and is entirely walkable — a tree-lined avenue in El Golf where virtually every building houses a restaurant, wine bar, or café. An evening stroll down Isidora is the easiest and most rewarding dining option for guests of The Ritz-Carlton.

Zone Two
MUT — Mercado Urbano Tobalaba
📍 Av. Tobalaba 1838, Providencia 🚶 Walkable or 5 min by Uber from The Ritz-Carlton

A contemporary multi-level urban market concept that has quickly become one of Santiago's most exciting food destinations — curated local producers, independent coffee roasters, and genuinely excellent restaurants spread across several floors, including a rooftop terrace. An excellent destination for lunch, an early dinner, or a casual evening out.

Casual

The official branch of the legendary institution of the Chilean sandwich — lomitos and churrascos cut to order on housemade bread, with a heritage that places it among the most iconic sandwich counters in the country. The ideal stop for any international visitor wanting to experience elevated Chilean street food at its finest.

Alonso de Córdova & Nueva Costanera
📍 Vitacura 🚗 5–10 min by taxi from The Ritz-Carlton

The gastronomic heart of Santiago — a tree-lined corridor of acclaimed chefs and concepts, from elegant Chilean seafood houses to world-class Nikkei cuisine. Together with Nueva Costanera, they form the city's finest dining mile, also incorporating the best of CV Galería along the way.

A note for our guests

Zone 1 (Isidora Goyenechea) and Zone 2 (MUT) are entirely walkable from The Ritz-Carlton. For dinner at upscale venues (Karai, Osaka, La Calma, Carnal), a call ahead the same day is advisable on weekends. Uber and taxis are readily available throughout El Golf and Vitacura — your hotel concierge can arrange a trusted driver. Prices in Chilean pesos (CLP); a generous dinner with wine ranges from USD 35–60 at moderate venues and USD 80–150+ at upscale addresses. Major credit cards accepted everywhere.

🇨🇱 Look for the Chilean flag icon to identify restaurants offering an authentic taste of local cuisine and tradition.
